Alberts 3 and 5 go to the shared space and set the table. Through the rest of the piece, all objects need to be attached to surfaces when they are not being handled. For example, food containers are strapped to the tabletop with cords. Utensils might snap into special clips on the edge of the table. Etc. The point is to communicate the constraints of and work associated with living in zero gravity without trying to convey the illusion that there actually is no gravity.
